Sushi Instructions

  • Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Tuna sushi grade
  • Soy Sauce and Wasabi
  • Pickled Ginger
  1. Wash rice
  2. 1 to 1 ratio of rice and water in steamer and cook.
  3. add rice vinegar to rice to liking.
  4. put rice on seaweed and spread to edges. Leave 2 inches of space on bottom.
  5. slice fish thin at angle, so pieces are about 1.5 inches wide
  6. place fish on top of rice opposite of the edge with no rice
  7. roll tightly into a roll
  8. cut ends off for presentation and cut the roll into 2 inch slices
  9. serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

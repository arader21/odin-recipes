Burger

Burger Instructions

  • Buns
  • 80/20 burger meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • American Cheese
  • Sliced Pickles
  • Ketchup and Mustard
  1. Toast buns in oven add cheese for last 2 min.
  2. Cook burger 4 min a side on medium high heat or 375 on grill.
  3. Put burger together. Bun, cheese, lettuce, tomato, burger meat, and onions and pickels on bottom.
